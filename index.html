About


Tradition since 1889

We are passionate about bringing the vibrant and diverse flavors of Latin cuisine to your table. Our journey began with a love for the rich culinary traditions found across the Caribbean, from the Savoryness of Dominican Republic to the savory delights of Puerto Rico. With a commitment to authenticity and innovation, we aim to create an unforgettable dining experience for every guest.
